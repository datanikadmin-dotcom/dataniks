Our Data Analytics Process

A simple, transparent process designed to take your data from messy to actionable — typically within 2–4 weeks.

1. Discovery & Planning

We start with a consultation to understand your business goals, challenges, and data sources.

  • Free consultation call
  • Data review
  • Define project scope

2. Data Cleaning & Preparation

Your data is cleaned, structured, and prepared for accurate analysis.

  • Fix missing or inconsistent data
  • Remove duplicates
  • Build data pipelines

3. Analysis & Insights

We analyze your data to uncover trends, patterns, and key business insights.

  • KPI analysis
  • Trend identification
  • Custom reporting

4. Dashboard & Delivery

You receive clear dashboards and reports along with guidance on how to use them.

  • Power BI dashboards
  • Final reports
  • Walkthrough & support

Typical Timeline

Week 1: Discovery & data review

Week 2: Data cleaning & preparation

Week 3: Analysis & dashboard development

Week 4: Final delivery & revisions

What We Need From You

Access to your data (Excel, database, etc.)

Clear business goals or questions

Feedback during review stages
